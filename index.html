PG dissertation

A PG dissertation is a long piece of academic writing that is required for completion of a postgraduate degree. It is a research project that the student conducts under the supervision of a faculty member. The topic of the dissertation is chosen by the student and should be related to their field of study.

  • Choose a topic that you are interested in and passionate about.
  • Start early and give yourself plenty of time to research and write.
  • Work closely with your supervisor and get their feedback on your work regularly.
  • Be organized and keep track of your progress.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help when you need it.
